## 2026-04-14 - [Add ARIA labels and focus states to icon-buttons]
 **Learning:** Icon-only interactive elements (like floating action buttons, modal dismisses, and chat inputs) must have explicit `aria-label` attributes and keyboard `focus-visible` states to support non-mouse users and screen readers properly.
 **Action:** Always ensure that icon-only buttons include descriptive ARIA labels and distinct focus states.
