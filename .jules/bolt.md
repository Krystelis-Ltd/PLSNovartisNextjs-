## 2024-05-18 - [Memoizing derived state during high-frequency updates]
**Learning:** The Dashboard component has a 100ms interval for an extraction timer `extractionTimeMs` that forces the entire component to re-render 10 times per second during document extraction. Because derived states like `promptData` (from `extractPrompts`) and `currentFetchedAnswers` (from filtering and reducing the `extractionFeed` array) were calculated inline without memoization, these expensive calculations ran on every tick, causing UI stutter and blocking the main thread during rapid re-renders.
**Action:** Always wrap expensive derived states in `useMemo` when a component relies on high-frequency state updates (like timers or progress bars), specifically to isolate those calculations from routine, unrelated re-renders.
